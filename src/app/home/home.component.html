Welcome to the UiTM International Conference on Mathematical Sciences 2024 (UICMS 2024)

We are delighted to invite you to the UiTM International Conference on Mathematical Sciences 2024 (UICMS2024), a prestigious event held in conjunction with the 3rd International Conference on Mathematical Sciences (ICMS 2024) and the 3rd Computational Models and Technologies (CMT2024). This conference is set to be a milestone event, unfolding on the 12th and 13th of October 2024.

UICMS 2024 aims to bring together a diverse group of thought leaders, innovators, and practitioners from around the world. We are focusing on the critical role of mathematics in the current age of intelligence and analytics, emphasizing the bridge between theoretical advancements and practical applications. This gathering is a unique opportunity for academics, professionals, researchers, scholars, and students to engage in a rich exchange of ideas, experiences, and expertise.

Our conference, through its expertly curated sessions and discussions, seeks to enhance collaborative efforts among academic institutions, especially between UiTM and its MoU partners, and to foster a culture of high-quality scholarship and research.

Join us for an enriching experience that promises to expand your horizons, provide valuable insights, and offer networking opportunities with peers and experts in the field of mathematics and its applications. We look forward to your participation in making UICMS 2024 a resounding success.

Conference Theme

Mathematics in the Age of Intelligence and Analytics: Bridging Theory and Applications

Objectives

  1. To provide a broad platform for academics, professionals, researchers, scholars, and students in Malaysia and around the world to interact and share knowledge, experience, and expertise in the field of mathematics and its applications.
  2. To enhance collaborative cooperation between UiTM and MoU partners.
  3. To serve as a catalyst for a culture of scholarship based on quality by encouraging academic community involvement in research, writing, and other scholarly activities.
  4. To create a forum for collaboration among international academic members for universal benefit.
  5. To provide motivation and opportunities for researchers to discuss and present the latest research findings in the field of mathematics and its applications.
  6. To produce high-impact publications through co-authorship for joint publications.
