Decision Making & Fuzzy System

Time: 9am - 5pm (GMT+8)
Platform: Microsoft Teams

Speakers:
  1. Dr. Nor Hanimah Kamis
  2. Dr. Tajul Rosli Razak
Register

Discover the theory of fuzzy systems and their practical applications in this interactive workshop. Participants will gain hands-on experience in designing and implementing fuzzy decision-making models using the Matlab Toolbox, Juzzy Online in Java, and the FuzzyR package in R. This workshop provides practical insights into creating intelligent and interpretable systems, with opportunities to apply these techniques to various decision-making challenges, including medical diagnosis, selection and evaluation tasks, and agricultural decision-making, among others.

Integrating Mathematics & Machine Learning

Date: 10 October 2024
Time: 9am - 5pm (GMT+8)
Platform: Microsoft Teams

Speaker:
  1. Dr. Mohd Razif Shamsuddin
Register

The workshop will begin with an overview of machine learning and its key concepts, emphasizing the mathematical foundations necessary for understanding and implementing machine learning algorithms. The workshop then will delved into linear regression, covering both the theoretical aspects, mathematical formulation, assumptions and practical implementation (coding and evaluating models). This workshop session will also include hands-on activities to apply the concepts that is learned. Participants will explore how to integrate mathematical principles with machine learning applications through case studies and an interactive project. Participants gained practical experience in applying linear regression to real-world data. The workshop will finally concluded with a discussion on specific topics and an open Q&A session, providing participants with a comprehensive understanding of how mathematics and machine learning intersect and how to further develop their skills. This workshop structure ensures a balance between theoretical understanding, practical application, and hands-on experience, giving participants a solid foundation in integrating mathematics with machine learning.
